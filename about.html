About

My name is Artem, and I am an Information Technology student at North Metropolitan TAFE. I have a strong interest in software development, web technologies, and problem-solving through programming. Throughout my studies, I have gained experience working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, C#, SQL, and modern development tools.

I enjoy creating practical applications and websites that combine functionality with clean and user-friendly design. During my projects, I have worked with databases, APIs, version control systems, and responsive web development principles. I am constantly looking for opportunities to improve my technical skills and learn new technologies that can help me become a more effective developer.

In addition to my studies, I enjoy working on personal projects that allow me to apply what I have learned in real-world scenarios. These projects help me strengthen my understanding of software architecture, user experience, and application development. I value attention to detail, continuous learning, and writing maintainable code.

My goal is to build a successful career in the IT industry, where I can contribute to meaningful projects, continue developing my skills, and work alongside experienced professionals. I am motivated, reliable, and enjoy both independent work and collaboration within a team environment.
